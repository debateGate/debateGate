debateGate.net isn't particularly interested in collecting your information.

Your account is your Google Account, and Google has its own privacy policy and terms/conditions and collects its own information, but we only store your email, real name (or, optionally, username), and a link to your current avatar in our database. Google Adsense is based off of user information that is all stored on Google's servers, so our ads are also based off of Google's privacy policy. debateGate.net is currently not usable without a Google Account.

The only other user information we gather is via Google Analytics, to track views/bounce rate and the like.

We do not send spam email -- all emails we send should be related to various debate events, like when someone joins your debate. These can be turned off, if you wish, in the settings menu.
